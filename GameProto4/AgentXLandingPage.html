The Adventures of Agent X

Gather household objects as you and your team travel through different dimensions to save the universe!

Rules

Webcam Set-up

Once you have your team on a web call and each agent has the webpage open, everyone click START! to begin your journey!

About the Project

This multimedia project combines the elements of a webpage, a users access to webcams, as well as real life elements within their physical location. This form of game allows for highly interactive gameplay and continues to push the boundaries of multimedia during the COVID-19 Pandemic.
